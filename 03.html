786 الرَّحِيمُ 3

Ar-RaheemMost Merciful

He is the source of infinite mercy and beneficence who rewards with eternal gifts. The One who uses his bounties for the good.

If recited daily after every salaah 100 times, the reciter will be safeguarded against all calamities, maladies and the entire creation will become affectionate towards him.

← Insha Allah 🤲 Ameen. →

Jumuah Mubarak..
