786 الرَّحْمَنُ 2

Ar-Rahmaan

He is the One who wills mercy and good for all creation,
at all times.
He pours upon all creation infinite bounties.

←
→

If this Ism is recited 100 times daily after every salaah,
if Allah (SWT) wills,
hard-heartedness and negligence will be removed from the reader's heart.


Insha Allah 🤲 Ameen.
